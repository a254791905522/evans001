Privacy

Effective date: April 11, 2026

Summary

Pack It Perfect is built so you can enjoy sorting puzzles without handing over personal data through the game itself. The application does not collect personal information from you for its own use.

No in-app data collection
What information does the game collect?
The game does not collect personal data such as your name, email address, or contacts. Gameplay does not rely on creating an account inside the app for these purposes.
Analytics, advertising, and trackers
The app does not embed analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or cross-app trackers for collecting your personal data. What you see in Apple’s App Privacy labels for Pack It Perfect is the authoritative summary for the store build.
Apple and your device
Downloading and updating the app happens through Apple’s services. Apple may process information according to its own policies and your device settings (for example, App Store account and system diagnostics). We do not control Apple’s systems.
Children
The game is rated for general audiences. If you are a parent or guardian and have questions about privacy on your child’s device, Apple provides parental controls and privacy settings in iOS and iPadOS.
Changes to this page
We may update this privacy summary when the app or legal requirements change. The effective date at the top will be revised when material updates are posted.

For the official store listing, system requirements, and Apple’s own summary of app privacy labels, see the Pack It Perfect page on the App Store.
