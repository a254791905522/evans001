Swing Army Rope Run is a fast‑paced action game about timing, momentum, and control. Use a single rope to swing, leap, and glide through dynamic levels while keeping your tiny army together.

Swing Army
Time each swing, avoid traps, and gather your troops. One rope. Dozens of tiny soldiers. Endless ways to get through.
Tactile rope swinging

Simple one-touch controls with satisfying physics. Every tap, hold, and release changes the path of your entire squad.

Dynamic levels

Levels are filled with moving platforms, traps, and clever obstacles that reward observation and precise timing.

Play anywhere

Designed for short sessions and longer runs alike. Play offline and pick up where you left off, wherever you are.
